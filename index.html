Air Guitar

A project that produces a piano sound according to the relative distance of both hands in a video input in real time
The scale is determined by calculating the distance of the skeleton points of both hands derived from the OpenPose library. On/off switch is also possible by determining the shape of the hand by inputting the skeleton point of the hand into the neural network.

A project that recognizes a vehicle and its license plate in the input photo
